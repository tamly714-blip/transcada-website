About Tran Controls Scada Solutions

TCSS was created to provide dependable SCADA, PLC, automation, and control system support for customers who need practical technical solutions.

Our background includes decades of experience in software systems, databases, remote access, industrial controls, field troubleshooting, and mission-critical operations.

We believe good automation should be understandable, maintainable, and reliable for the people who operate it every day.
